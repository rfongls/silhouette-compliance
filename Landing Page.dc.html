Stateless by design

Your documents are never stored

Your policies and evidence are held in memory for a single analysis, then discarded. Here is exactly where the line sits:

Never stored
✕
Your source documents
Held in memory for one run, then discarded. Never written to disk.
✕
Patient & client records
Never collected. Assessments read policies and configurations, not records.
✕
Your data for AI training
Analysis runs on a zero-retention tier. Prompts and outputs are not kept.
What we keep
✓
